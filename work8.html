Roku's Flashback App

This group project was developed for a Multimedia Authoring class at Fanshawe College.

Objective

The briefing given to us was to develop from scratch a fictional app for a Roku "channel" called Flashback, focused on 50s, 60s, 70s, 80s and 90s content. We had to include both video and audio from these periods of time. It needed to be responsive, and created as a mobile first app.

Result

We developed the logo trying to keep the style from the past decades but add a touch of modern, so it would grab the attention of all kinds of audiences. We kept the Roku purple to keep some of the brand identity and added yellow and green for contrast. We decided to keep the layout simple and clean, with a focus on the videos and audio covers.
