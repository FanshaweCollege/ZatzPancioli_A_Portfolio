Eco Culinarius

This group project was developed for a Visual Communication class at Fanshawe College.

Objective

The class was given a briefing that focused on creating a logo and some basic visual communication for a chain of fast food restaurants that sells organic and natural foods. We were given the name Eco Culinarius, but the rest was up to each group to decide.

The concept

The main concept of our brand is sustainability, so the first time you order at the restaurant you pay a little more and get your food in a glass jar, you take it home and bring it with you every other time. The whole brand would revolve around that idea, the clutery and napkins all being recyclable.


Creative Process

For the logo, we chose a "handwritten" tipography, to give a more relaxed and free touch that would suit the main concept. To add a little more to the logo, we played around with some shapes and colors and decided to use green as the primary color and use the deconstruction of a jar.

The Result

After finishing the logo, we developed the rest of the brand identity. We chose a pattern for the backgrounds, focusing on the tiles we would have on the wall of our restaurants, and the eco friendly fabric we would use for our uniforms and aprons. Eco Culinarius would also be comprised of a mobile app, so you could order online and check out our menu and promotions.
