Purevolume Website

This group project was an Integrated Project developed for the third term of the Interactive Media Design program at Fanshawe College.

Objective

This project was a seven day assignment comprised of the revitalization of the Purevolume website. The website had the goal of devilering news about music, fashion, entertainment and pop culture, and we had to reinvent it to be more complete and modern.

Result

We decided to keep the logo as it was, and some of the color palette, but change the overall feel of the website. We kept the dark grey used on the menu of the original site, but applied it to the background, keeping the rest mostly black and white with a touch of yellow. We left the color to the important details, the advertisements and some of the imagery, bringing a more modern and clean look.
